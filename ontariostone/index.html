About Us

Ontario Stone Corporation is a privately owned stone shipping industry located in Cleveland, Ohio. We handle around 1 million tons of stone per year, all shipped out via truck for the local market. Our onsite storage capacity is 400,000 tons.

Stone is shipped in via lake ship from Ohio and Michigan.
